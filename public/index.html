process

Design isn't a one step process.

  • Strategy — The napkin sketch phase. The rough draft of the project.

  • Plan — The wire frame phase. The architecture or bones of the project. Determining what goes where and why.

  • Build — The construction phase. The project comes to life with color, typography and functionality.

  • Promote — The marketing phase. From SEO to social media, this is how you get noticed
